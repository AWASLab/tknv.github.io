*WARNING* It may damage your property, doing under your responsibility.

Prepare:
  1. Using android phone (armel cpu, it means a bit old android).
  2. Rooted.
  3. Installed busybox and terminal app.
  4. mkdir chroot-btsync in sdcard dir at armel android.
  5. Download btsync armel pack; here
  6. Make run script; here
  7. Set up by URL http://localhost:8888 on your android.
If you anxious, please check MD5 hash of btsync binary and exchange libs from debian squeeze 2.6.36. Yet I did not sync any file, just see ps and see setting page on browser.
